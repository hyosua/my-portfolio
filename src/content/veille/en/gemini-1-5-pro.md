At the Google I/O 2024 conference, a major announcement was made: the introduction of Gemini 1.5 Pro. This next-generation model promises significant advances in long-context understanding and multimodal capabilities.

One of its most impressive features is a context window of up to 1 million tokens, enabling the analysis of entire codebases, long documents, or hours of video in a single pass.
